Welcome to Common.

How to Join

The only way to join is to be invited, in person, by an active player. Here's a schedule. You can also check here to see what's happening right now.

What to do

If you are an active player, there are 3 things you can do here:

  1. Invite new players
  2. Strengthen your connections
  3. Do nothing and watch your connections dwindle away

Useful things to know

  • You have a limited amount of life-force you can use to invite players or reinforce connections.
  • Your life-force is replenished daily by the Giver as long as you keep your Common strong.
  • The Destroyer also visits daily and decays connections and life-force at random.
